Husky summary

Siberian huskies are intelligent, active, and loyal companions. Although they can be challenging to train, they require plenty of exercise. Because they are such strong animals, they should be trained early and carefully, and they may not respond well to harsh training methods. They are excellent guard dogs, but they are not good watchdogs.

why huskies are so dramatic

Huskies are dramatic because of a combination of genetic traits, and human feedback. As an inherently vocal and stubborn breed, Huskies are genetically predispondent to communicate vocally when not wanting to do something or feeling something strongly.
